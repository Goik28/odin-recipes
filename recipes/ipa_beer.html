Indian Pale Ale Beer

There is a lot of types of beer but the Indian Pale Ale has a special place among them.

The IPA beer has strong alcohol content and a classic bitternes.

Brew 10 litres

brew time: 14 days

Ingredients

  • 12l of water
  • 3kg of Pale Malt
  • 1 sache of Citra hops
  • 1 sache of Summit hops
  • 1 sache of Topaz hops
  • 75g of sugar
  • 1 Lemon

Steps

Brewing

  1. Heat the water to 72 celsius degrees
  2. Add the malt
  3. Cool it down to 65C degrees and stir for 1 hour
  4. Heat it gradually 1C per minute until it reaches 75C.
  5. Start the recirculation process for 20 minutes
  6. Transfer the moist to another pot
  7. Add the "Citra" hop and boil for 40 minutes
  8. Add the "Summit" hop and let it boil for 10 more minutes
  9. add the "Topaz" hop and boil for 1 hour
  10. Chill the brewed liquid to 30C
  11. Let it ferment for 10 days at 20C
  12. Change the temperature to 12C for 4 days
  13. It's ready to bottle

Bottling

  1. Boil 250ml of water for 2 minutes
  2. Add the sugar and 3 to 5 droplets of lemon
  3. Put 25ml of this mix in each bottle
  4. Add the brew and bottle it
  5. Let the bottles rest for 15 days
  6. Put them in the freezer and call your friends to drink it
