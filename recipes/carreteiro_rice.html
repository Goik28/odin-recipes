Carreteiro Rice

Carreteiro rice is a popular recipe in the south part of Brazil.

It's common made from leftovers of another famous Brazillian dish: Churrasco.

Serves 4 portions

Cook time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 spoon of oil
  • 1/2 cup of small chopped cubes of bacon
  • 1 crushed garlic
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 200g of shredded cooked meat
  • 2 medium tomatoes without seeds and chopped in small cubes
  • 1,5 cups of rice
  • 1 spoon of salt
  • 3 cups of boiling water
  • 1 spoon of herbs

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat the oil and fry the bacon until it is golden collored
  2. Add the garlic, the onion and the meat and stew them for some minutes
  3. Add the tomatoes, the rice, salt and the boiling water
  4. Partially close the lid and cook it from 10 to 15 minutes, or until the rice dry and become cooked
  5. Close the lid and let it rest for 5 minutes
  6. Add the herbs and it's ready to serve
