Classic Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic dish from Italy.

It can be made of diverse fillings, this one is with beef and pork.

Serves 6 portions

Cook time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500ml of tomatoes extract
  • 200g of shredded cooked meat
  • 100g of shredded sausage, without cape
  • 100g of fresh ricotta
  • 100g of parmesan cheese
  • 100g of mussarella cheese
  • 1/2 spoon of basil
  • 1/2 spoon of chopped onions
  • 1/2 spoon of thyme
  • 1/2 spoon of oregano
  • 1/2 spoon of minced garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

Cooking

  1. In a bowl, mix the sausage, basil, thyme, oregano, onion and the garlic
  2. Let it rest
  3. In a hot pot, fry the beef and the preview mix
  4. If needed, add a bit of olive oil to avoid burning it
  5. Add the tomato extract and let it boil for 2 minutes
  6. Let it rest
  7. In another bowl, mix all the cheeses

Mounting

  1. Anoit a mold with olive oil
  2. Line the mold with sheet dough
  3. Put 5 spoons of the mixed cheese
  4. Put 3 spoons of the filling
  5. Repeat the process until you spend all the ingredients
  6. Finalize it with cheese
  7. Cook it in a pre-heated oven for 25 minutes
  8. Serve it
